Introduction

We are living in a fast moving society, everything is being digitalized in order to compete with time. Smartphones are a perfect example of technological advancement since they allow users to perform various things while being in one location without moving. With the introduction of smartphones, Android has become the dominant mobile operating system, powering over 1.2 million devices globally.In 2016, the World Health Organization projected that around 1.35 million people died as a result of road accidents worldwide, which equates to approximately one fatality every 25 seconds. The bulk of these incidents occur as a result of inattention while driving, disobeying traffic signs, and driving when drowsy. The goal of this article is a smart driver assistant that will aid drivers when they need extra assistance while driving and prevent undesired accidents and deaths.

The solution will be accessible as a mobile application that collects data from the surroundings in real time using some external devices. Fundamentally, the application could well be managed through speech and occasionally manually. This system is highly important in the user's everyday life since it allows the user to create task reminders at several places, giving him an idea and alerting him about chores he has to do at a certain location. Furthermore, the user may operate the application through voice, and the device will track the carâ€™s location/movement and notify the user using alarming system when arrives at the pinned location.

In addition, the application will guarantee that the motorist does not miss a traffic sign. The application continually captures every traffic symbol along the way in real time and provides the information to the driver through voice. It will help to reduce the unwanted costs that paid as fines by the drivers and mainly reduce accidents caused by forgetting the road signs. Detecting Drowsiness level of the user and suggesting to rest on the nearby parking slots, sometime playing songs to wind up the drowsy feel is also another feature of the integrated application. Also, People can find the free parking slots while driving by searching the available parking slots via voice, in the large shopping malls, theatres,large complexes and other important areas before reaching those destinations.
