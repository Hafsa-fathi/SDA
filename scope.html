Main Objectives

The main objective is to come up with an effective solution to solve the major problems of driver by developing a smart driver assistant mobile application software with intelligent voice assistant using artificial intelligence, deep learning, machine learning and convolutional neural network. As an application software that should be applied as an alerting system for avoiding the accidents due to the drowsiness and careless of road signs. Application should be an assistant for the driver which should be reminding the pinned locations in the map when the user reaching the certain radius of the destination. Moreover, the mobile application should recognize the voice/command of the user and performs the needs to the driver/user.

Sub Objectives

Registration of users and property

  • To register on the platform, users and legal officers need to visit the application and need to make registration. On clicking the submit button, the user will get verified through email and from there, the user will be redirected to the login page.
  • To login, users must provide their Username and Password.
  • register the property, choose the Add Land tab from the dashboard and fill all the details related to the property and owner. After submitting the form, the user's application is then verified by the government authority(Legal officer).
Validation from government authority In this step, government authority (Legal Officer) audits the user's land details and has the right to approve or decline their application. During the time of Registration of land, users have to upload the legal documents of land so that the government and the buyer can verify it. If rejected, the user has to submit a new application, and it cannot be available for other users to buy. And if it gets accepted, then the owner of the land has the option to make their land available to sell. When Government accepts or rejects the request, it will notify the user in the user's dashboard. Transaction between both the parties This step has several stages involved. Following are the steps required:- Making the land available Once the Government is approving it, the landowner has the option to make their land available to other users. Sending requests to the landowner to purchase When the land is available to buy, the buyer moves toward their dashboard, and in the Buy land section, they search for the property and send a request to the landowner to buy it. Viewing the request, the Land proprietor views the buyer's requests and after communicating with them, plans whether to allow them to buy the land or not. They have the option to accept or decline the request. Processing the request Once the landowner views the requester address and if it is found to be the right one, then the seller accepts the request. Buying the property Once the request is approved, the buyer can now buy the property. Once the seller accepts the buyerâ€™s request, the legal officer will be notified. A legal officer is the person who needs to transfer the ownership of the previous landowner to the new owner. This ownership transaction will be run through a smart contract. The transaction process is conducted in the form of the smart contract, which ensures that the process is immutable, secured and digitized. No one in between can tamper the data, and authenticity is maintained throughout the process. There is no human error involved. Paperwork will be reduced, and most importantly, everything will become transparent. Chances of fraud cases will be reduced. The public ledger can be used in case there is any doubt regarding the land ownership claim.
